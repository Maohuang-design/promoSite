THE INFECTION SPREADS

In a world where power and privilege dominate, four men live their lives benefiting from a deeply patriarchal society. The more they indulge in their dominance—controlling others, belittling women, and reinforcing outdated traditions—the stronger a mysterious virus grows inside them.

When the infection reaches its peak, they transform into mindless, violent zombies, spreading the virus to others like them.

The virus feeds on privilege.

As chaos engulfs the city, a group of survivors discovers that the only cure is self-awareness and change. But can those who have always thrived on power truly let go of it? Or is the virus destined to consume everything in its path?

STAGE 1: DENIAL

The virus begins to take hold as the host refuses to acknowledge their toxic behavior.

STAGE 2: AGGRESSION

As the infection spreads, the host becomes increasingly violent in defense of their privilege.

STAGE 3: TRANSFORMATION

The final stage where humanity is lost and the host becomes a vessel for spreading the infection.
